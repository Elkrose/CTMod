Simple Mod to crash on top of the backgrounds without worrying about losing your backgrounds during updates etc.

The freedom to customize is yours. Simply drop this mod into your mod folder and watch as it seamlessly replaces the core background without overwriting any crucial files. 

Don't like some of the images? No problem - just delete the unwanted ones from the VTMod/CTimages/ folder. 

Feeling creative? Go ahead and create your own custom backgrounds to personalize your experience. The possibilities are endless.
